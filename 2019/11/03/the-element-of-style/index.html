Elementary Rules Of Usage

1. Form the possessive singular of nouns by add ‘s.

不论单数词语的结尾是什么都遵循这个规则。经常出现的错误是以 s 结尾的词语，比如应该是 Charles’s friend 而不是 Charles’ friend，Charles’s friend 指的是许多个 Charle 的朋友。唯一的例外是以 -es、-is 结尾的古代专有名词，比如说 Jesus'、Moses'，但是通常会使用 the laws of Moses 代替 Moses‘ laws。

2. In a series of three or more terms with a single conjunction, use a comma after each term except the last.

比如： red, white, and blue He opened the letter, read it, and made a note of its contents. 不过公司名称中，最后一个逗号经常省略：Little, Brown and Company

3. Enclose parenthetic expressions between commas.

这一条规则应用起来比较复杂。首相我们来理解一下什么是 parenthetic expressions：A parenthetical expression is a word or words added to a sentence without changing the meaning or grammar of the original sentence. Parenthetical expressions give extra information but are not essential. You can add and remove a parenthetical and the sentence works just the same. 比如：The best way to see a country, unless you are pressed for time, is to travel on foot。如果当插入的词或短语仅轻微地中断行文并不太影响整个句子的，我们也可以省略这两个逗号，但是绝不能只省略一个逗号。

错的 对的
Mary’s husband, Mike paid us a visit Mary’s husband Mike paid us a visit
  • 日期 February to July, 1992 April 6, 1986 Wednesday, November 14 1990 30 December 2019 当使用“日期-月份-年份”的顺序表达时，逗号可以省略
  • A name or a tittle in direct address is parenthetical. 比如： Well, Susan, this is a fine mess you are in.
  • 缩写词 etc.、i.e. 、e.g. 比如 Letters, packages, etc., should go there.
  • 人名之后的头衔 Rachel Simonds, Attorney
  • 人名之后表示身份的限制性短语不应该被逗号隔开 The novelist Jane Austen Billy the kid
  • 限制性定语不用逗号，非限制行定语用逗号分开 限制性定语: People who live in glass houses shounn’t thrown stones. 非限制性定语: In 1976, when Napoleon was born, Corsica had but recently been acquired by France.

4. Place a comma before conjunction introducing an independent clause.

由连词链接的两个分句，连词前需用逗号分开。比如： The situation is perilous, but there is still on chance of escape. 连词包括 but、and、as(because 的意思)、for、nor、while（and at the same time 的意思）。 两个分句的主语一致并且只出现一次时，连词 but 前用逗号隔开。如果 and 连接两个分句关系紧密，逗号可以省略。比如： I have heard the arguments, but am still unconvinced He has had several years' experience and is thoroughly competent.

5. Do not join independent clauses with a comma.

如果我们想把两个语法完整的句子组成一个句子的时候，用分号连接而不是逗号。语法上同样正确的还有用句号连接和用连词和逗号连接，但是用分号连接更简洁而且能表达两个句子之间的紧密关系。如果使用副词连接，仍然需要分号。 I had never been in the place before; besides, it was dark as a tomb.

分号 句号 逗号
Mary Shelley’s works are entertaining; they are full of engaging ideas. Mary Shelley’s works are entertaining. They are full of engaging ideas. Mary Shelley’s works are entertaining, for they are full of engaging ideas.
例外：如果各个分句都很短并且形式相似，或者整个句子的语气很轻松像是在对话，那么用逗号更合适。比如
Man proposes, God disposes.
I hardly knew him, he was so changed.

6. Do not break sentences in two.

换言之就是不要用句号代替逗号。

错的 对的
I met them on a Cunard liner many years ago. Coming home from Liverpool to New York. I met them on a Cunard liner many years ago, coming home from Liverpool to New York.

7. Use a colon after an independent clause to introduce a list of particulars, an appositive, an amplification, or an illustrative quotation.

  • Introduce a list of particulars 冒号不能置于动词和其宾语或介词和其宾语之间。
    错的 对的
    Your dedicated whittler requires: a knife, a piece of wood and a back porch. Your dedicated whittler requires three props: a knife, a piece of wood and a back porch
  • Introduce amplification. 比如： But even so, there was a directness and dispatch about animal burial: there was no stopover in the undertaker’s foul parlor, no wreath or spray.
  • Introduce quotation. 比如： The squalor of the streets reminded her of a line from Oscar Wilde: “We are all in the gutter, but some of us are looking at the stars.

8. Use a dash to set off an abrupt break or interruption and to announce a long appositive or summary.

破折号比逗号有更强的分隔作用，但不如分号正式，不如括号严谨。只有当普通标点都不合适的时候才使用破折号。 He first thought on getting out of bed–if he had any thought at all–was to get back in again.

错的 对的
Violence–the kind you see on television–is not honestly violent–there lies its harm. Violence, the kind you see on television, is not honestly violent. There lies its harm.

9. The number of the subject determines the number of the verb.

  • 主语和谓语动词之间的词不影响谓语动词 The bittersweet flavor of youth–its trials, its joys–is not soon forgotten.
  • 在 “one of …” 之后的关系从句或者类似表达中，关系代词才是主语，因此从句中的谓语动词的数由关系代词决定,而不是由 one 决定是单数。 One of those people who are never ready on time.
  • Each、either、everyone、everybody、neither、nobody、someone 为主语时谓语动词用单数形式。
  • 当 none 作"no one"或"not one"理解时，谓语动词用单数形式。 None of us is perfect. 当 none 指一个以上的人或物时，谓语动词常用复数形式。 None are so fallible as those who are sure they’re right. (没有很理解为什么 none 在这里指一个以上）。
  • 由 and 连接的两个或以上的名词组成的复合动词一般用需要复数形式的动词。某些约定俗成的固定搭配因为不可分割而视为整体，故使用单数形式的动词。 The walrus and the carpenter were walking close at hand. Bread and butter was all she served.
  • 单数主语，在通过with、as well as、in additon to、except、together with和no less than连接了其它的名词的情况下，仍然使用用单数形式谓语动词。
  • 有些词虽然是复数形式但是其实是单数词 Politics is an art, not science.

10. Use the proper case of pronoun.

  • 正确使用人称代词的主格和宾格形式，主要看他们代替的词是主语还是宾语。 Will Jane or he be hired, do you think? Give this work to whoever looks idle. 不用whomever因为它是looks idle的主语，而整个whoever looks idle是to的宾语。 Virgil is the candidate who we think will win.who是will win的主语 Virgil is the candiate whom we hope to elect. whom是hope to elect的宾语。
  • 比较级中的代词取决于它是已经叙述过或者省略的动词的主语还是宾语。为了避免歧义，最好不要省略动词。 Sandy writes better than I. (Than I write) Polly loves cake more than she loves me. I think Horace admires Jessica more than I do.
  • 反身代词不做主语。Blake and I (不能用myself) stayed at home.
  • 动名词用所有格形式，动词现在进行时用宾格形式。 Mother objected to our driving on icy road. They heard him singing in the shower. 实际使用的时候两者区别可能比较细微，要注意分析，比如： Do you mind me asking a question? vs. Do you mind my asking a question? 前者是你介不介意我而不是其他人问问题，后者是你介不介意问问题这件事。

11. A participial phras at the begining of a sentence must refer to the grammatical subject.

错的 对的
Walking slowly down the road, he saw a woman accompanied by two children. （此句中walking描绘主语he而不是woman) He saw a woman, accompanied by two children, walking slowly down the road.
On arriving Chicago, his friends met him at the station. On arriving Chicago, he was met at the station by his friends.
A soldier of proved valor, they entrusted him with the defense of the city. A solier of proved valor, he was entrusted with the defense of the city.

Elementary Principles of Composition

写作是一件很主观的事情。书里规则提供了帮助我们写出更好文章的起始点，但绝对不是终点，因此我们也不应该过分教条，就像此书出现的各种“例外”一样，我们也应该因地制宜。

12. Choose a suitable design and hold to it.

写文章也是一件需要计划的事情，计划好合适的结构、布局，然后执行它。

13. Make paragragh the unit of composition.

好分段是为了视觉美观和有逻辑的表达从而方便读者理解。必须谨记过长的段落或者频繁的分段都会给影响读者阅读。

  • 一般来说，一个主题包含若干论点的时候，每个论点应该自成一段，除非这个主题范围很小或者你打算写的比较简短，比如写一个书评或者总结。
  • 每一段的第一句要承担好它作用：点明论点或者承上启下。我们可以用一个词，比如：however、therefore、again，也可以用一两句话慢慢的引导或者过渡。
  • 写对话的时候一般每个人的话各成一段，除非刻意营造快速对话的情景。

14. Use the active voice.

主动语态一般更加直接、简洁、有力。下图中，右边的句子是更好的表达。
There were a great number of dead leaves lying on the ground Dead leaves covered the ground. Dead leaves covered the ground.
The reason he left college was that his health became impaired. Failing health compelled him to leave college.
At the dawn the crowing of a rooster could be heard. The cock’s crow came with dawn.

这并不是说我们要完全摈弃被动语态，而是要尽量选择更加简洁、合适的表达，因为通常越有力的表达也就越简洁。所谓的active voice,也取决于我们需要谁的voice。下面两句话中，如果我们的重点是dramatists of Restoration，我们就应该用左边的比表达，如果重点是modern readers，就应该用右边的。
Modern readers have little esteem for the dramastists of the Restoration. The dramastists of the Resotoration are little esteemed by modern readers.

15. Put statement in positive form.

表达需要肯定有力。含糊其辞，吞吐推诿的表达会让文章失去权威性和说服力。

  • not的使用应该是表达否定或对立而是不是推诿。下图中，右边的句子是更好的表达。
He was not very often on time. He usually came late.
He did not think that studying Latin was a sensible way to use one’s time. He thought the study of Latin a waste of time.
  • 即使表达否定，用肯定的形式也更好。 not honest –> dishonest did not rembember –> forgot did not have much confidence in –> distrusted
  • 把肯定和否定对立着放是一种很强的表达结构。 Ask not what your country can do for you–ask you can do for your country.
  • 带有多余的辅助词的句子显得作者充满犹豫,难以说服读者，因此只有真的不确定的时候才使用should、could、would、may、might等辅助词。
    If you would let us know your arrival, we would be very happy to arrange your transportation from the airport. If you will let us know your arrival, we shall be very happy to arrange your arrival.
    Plath may be ranked among thos poets who died young. Plath was one of those poets who died young.

16. Use definite, specific, concrete language.

具体、肯定、明确的文字比笼统、抽象、含糊的文字更吸引读者。（存在疑惑，抽象的文字在学术、商务的主题下应该是很流行的,感觉词条更应用于文学描绘。）

17. Omit needless words

还是那句话，有力表达要求简洁。不是说句子要短或者省略细节，而是要保证文章的每一个字都有意义–不要有多余的词语和句子。 下图左边是冗余的说法。
the question as to whether whether (the question whether)
there is no doubt but that no doubt (doubtless)
used for fuel purposes used for fuel
he is a man who … he …
in a hasty manner hastily
this is a subject that this subjet …
Her story is a strange one Her story is strange
the reason why is that because

the fact that 是个非常虚弱的表达，应该从所有句子中删掉。类似的词语还有case, character、nature。
owing to the fact that since(because)
in spite of the fact that though(although)
call your attention to the fact that remind you (notify you)
I was unaware of the fact that I was unaware that (did not know)
the fact that he had not succeeded his failure
the fact that I had arrived my arrival

which is, who is 也经常是多余的词语。His cousin, who is a member of the same firm.

避免啰嗦的方法是不要把复杂的想法写成一个行为（句子）接一个行为（句子），而是想办法精写成一句话。比如51个词的第一段话可以改写成26个词的第二段话。 Macbeth was very ambitious. This lead him to wish to become king of Scottland. The witches thold him that this wish of his would come true.The king of Scotland at this time was Duncan. Encouraged by his wife, Macbeth murdered Duncan. He was thus enabled to succeed Duncan as king. $\Longrightarrow$ Encouraged by his wife, Macbeth achieced his ambition and realized the prediction of the witches by murdering Duncan and becoming king of Scotland in his place.

18. Avoid a succession of loose sentences.

这里的loose sentences主要指由两个从句构成并且第二个从句由连词（and,but）或者非限制性关系代词(who, which, when, where, while)开头。句子过于紧凑和periodic（中心意思放在句尾的句子）会显得过于正式，加入loose sentences可以缓解读者的阅读压力，但是也要避免的通篇都是loose sentences。如果不慎出现了通篇都是loose sentence的情况,可以将部分句子替换成简单句、由分号连接两个从句构成的句子、两个从句构成地periodic的句子或者三个从句组成的句子。

Periodic Not Periodic
When I was shopping in the town yesterday, I saw Mike! I saw Mike when I was shopping in the town yesterday!

19. Express coordinate ideas in similar form.

这条规则类似于中文写作的排比结果。内容和功能相似的表达在结构也应该相似，这样能帮助读者意识到他们在内容和功能上的相似。比如下图中右边更好：
Formerly, science was taught by the textbook method, while now the laborotory method is employed. Forerly, science was taught by the textbook method; now it is taught by the laboratory method.
本着该原则，应用在一系列并列句所有成员的冠词或者介词要么只在第一项使用，要么在所有项使用。
错误 正确
——————————– ————————————————————–
in spring, summer or in winter in spring, winter or summer (in sping, in winter or in summer)
A time not for words but actions A time not for words but for actions

相关表达式（both, and;not, but; not only, but also; either, or; first, second, third; and the like)应该使用相同的语法结构。

错误 正确
It was both a long ceremony and very tedious. The ceremony was both long and tedious
My objections are, first, the injustice of the measure; second, that it is unconstitutional. My objections are, first, that the measure in unjust; second, that it is unconstituional.

20. Keep related words together.

  • 词在句子中的位置是表达他们关系的主要方法。相关的词和词组应该应该放在一起，不相关的应该隔开。不合理的位置摆放会导致歧义。
错误 正确
You can call your mother and tell her all about George’s taking you out to dinner for just two dollars. (读者会困惑到底是打电话$2还是晚饭$2) For just two dollars you can call your mother and tell her all about George’s taking you out to dinner.
  • 大多数情况下关系代词应该紧跟在先行词后面。如果先行词是词组，如果不会引起歧义，关系代词应该在词组的最后。
错误 正确
This is a portrait of Benjamin, grandson of William, who became President in 1889 This is a portrait of Benjamin, who became President in 1889. He was the grandson of William.
The superintendent of Chicago Division, who…
A proposal to amend the Sherman Act, which has been variously judged…(读者会困惑到底是proposal还是sherman act被judge） A proposal, which has been variously judged, to amend the Sherman Act …
  • 同位语中的名词可以放在先行词和介词中间。这样并不会带来歧义。 The Duke of York, his brother, who was …
  • 修饰词应该尽量靠近它所修饰的词。如果有多个修饰词，要正确安排它们的位以免发生产生错误的修饰。 He found only two mistakes.还是He only found two mistakes.

21. In summaries, keep to one tense

  • 概述戏剧行为用一般现在时。概述诗歌、故事或者小说时也可以用一般现在，除非使用用一般过去时更自然。
  • 如果概述使用一般现在时，之前的行为使用现在完成时；如果概述使用一般过去时，之前的行为使用过去完成时。
  • 作者应该保持时态统一。
  • 在转述他人文章或者演讲的时候不应过多使用类似 “he said”, “the speaker added”, “the author also thinks"的表述。更好的表达方式是在一开始就表明下文乃是概述，之后就不用浪费笔墨反复说明。
  • 在写文学评论或者文学诠释要避免写成概述。如果讨论涉及多部作品，最好不要按照时间先后顺序一个一个写，而是在开篇确立总的结论。

22. Place the emphatic words of a sentence at the end.

最适合作者想要强调的词和词组的位置是句尾。放在这个位置体现重要性的词和词语通常是logic predicate，也就是句子中的新信息。periodic sentence的有效性也来自于它突出了主句。

The steel is principally used for making razors, because of its hardness $\Longrightarrow$ Because of its hardness, this steel is used principally for making razors.

另一个体现重要性的位置是句首。任何不是主语的词语放在句首都能提到强调的作用。如果要特别强调主语，则要放在句尾。 Through the middle of the valley flowed a winding stream. 总之，需要强调的位置是在末端。同样的原则也适用于段落中的句子和文章中的段落。

A Few Matters of Form

Parentheses

当一个句子有部分在圆括号内，这句话的标点符号的位置仍然应该在括号之外。括号内的表达独立标点，如果句尾需要句号可以忽略（问号或者感叹号不能忽略）。

I went to her house yesterday (my thired attempt to see her), but she had left town. He declares (and why should we doubt his good faith?) that he is now certain of success

唯一一个例外是，如果整个句子都在括号内，我们就不用忽略句号了。 (When a wholly sentence detached expression or sentence is parenthesized, the final stop comes before the last mark of parenthesis.)

Quotations

  • 正式的引用（比如文件证明）用冒号和引号引导。 The United States Coast Pilot has this to say of the place:“Bracy Cove has a rocky and uneven bottom.”
  • 如果引用部分在语法上是同位语或动词的直接宾语，用逗号和引号引导。 I’m reminede of the advice of my neighbor, “Never worry about the weather.” My mom says, “Be prepared for tommorow.”
  • that引导的引文是间接引语，不用引号。 Keats declares that beauty is truth, truth beauty.
  • 如果引用一行或者一行以上的文字，为了区别之前的正文，引文可以另起一行并且缩进。引文也不用引号引导。 Wordsworth’s enthusiasm for the French Revolution was at first unbounded:     Bliss was it in that dawn to be alive,     But to be young was very heaven!

Title

书名一般是斜体加首字母大写。如果书名前使用所有格，把书名中的A或者The去掉。 A Tale of Two Cities $\Longrightarrow$ Dickens’s Tale of Two Cities

Misused Words and Expressions

很多误用的表达不是不好的英语而是不好的习惯，与其说是替换这些误用的表达，不如说是替换不清晰的、过于笼统的表达。书中提到了很多误用，这里只摘取了一些我曾经误用过的。

And/or. 这是一个容易导致不清晰表达的缩写
First of all, would an hornor system successfully reduce the amount of stealing and/or cheating? First of all, would an hornor system successfully reduce the amount of stealing or cheating or both?

As good or better than. 使用的时候需要重新排列句子。
My opinion is as good or better than his. My opinion is as good as his, or better.

Case. 很多时候我们cases的使用是多余的. 比如： In many cases, the rooms lacked air conditioniong. $\Longrightarrow$ Many rooms lacked air conditioning.

Compare.

  • Compare to 是指出或者暗示两个不同种事物之间的相似性。 Life has been compared to a battle or to a dramma. Paris has been compared to ancient Athena.
  • Compare with 是指出两个同种事物之间的不同性。 Congress can be compared with British Parliment. Paris may be compared with modern London.

Currently. 与动词一起使用来表达“现在”的意思是多余的。如果想要强调“现在”，最好用更加明确的有时间指向性的表达。 We are currently reviewing your profile $\Longrightarrow$ We are at this moment reviewing your profile.

He is a man who. 是一种非常多余的表达，详见 17. Omit needless words。

He is a man who is very lucky. $\Longrightarrow$ He is very lucky. China is a country that attracts vistors because of its history. $\Longrightarrow$ China attracts vistors because of its history.

Kind of. sort of. 口语中我们经常用 kind of 和 sort of 表达 rather 或者 something like，但是在写作或者正式表达时，最好还是用回它们字面上的意思。 Amber is a kind of fossil resin.

Less. 它容易和 fewer 误用。less 指的是 quantity 而 fewer 指的是 number。 His troubles are less than mine 意思是他的麻烦不想玩这么大（His troubles are not so great as mine。） His troubles are fewer than mine. 意思是他的麻烦数量上比我少 （His troubles are not so numerous as mine.)

Like. 不论是口语还是书面表达，经常有人会用 like 代替 as。比如 It smells good, like a baby should $\Longrightarrow$ It smells good, as a baby should. 虽然很多人这么用，但是我们还是要尽量避免这么表达。like在表达“像”的意思时，后面跟名字或者代词，如果右面跟从句应该用 as。

Personally 大多数情况下非常多余的词语。

Thanks in advance 这是表达我在邮件里还挺常用的。但它听着像作者在说：“不值得再给你写封邮件表达感谢。”更好的提要求的方法是“Will you please…“和”I shall be obliged”。之后如果还想表达感谢可以在写邮件表达。
